How this calculator works

The tool estimates a gram-per-kilogram range from your activity level and goal, based on ranges commonly cited in ISSN position stands and ACSM sports nutrition guidance, then multiplies that by your body weight to get a daily gram target. It also divides that target across your stated number of meals.

Why goal changes the number

Someone maintaining weight needs meaningfully less protein than someone in a calorie deficit trying to preserve muscle, or someone in a surplus actively building it. That's why this calculator asks for your goal instead of giving one flat number for everyone.

Limitations

This estimate doesn't account for pregnancy, certain medical conditions, or extreme body compositions. If you have kidney disease, liver disease, or another condition affecting protein metabolism, talk to your doctor before changing your intake.
